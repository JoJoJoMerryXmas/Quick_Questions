# AI Readiness Check

**The question:** Where does AI actually fit in your business — and what should you leave alone?

This is a 10–15 minute conversation that surfaces what's worrying you about AI, maps opportunities to your specific business, and gives you an AI Readiness Score. More importantly, it helps you understand why picking tools before understanding your business is the most common mistake founders make.

You get an AI Readiness Summary at the end: your score, your primary anxiety, and a sketch of where AI could have impact — grounded in what you actually told me, not a generic list.

---

## Install

1. Download [`ai-readiness-check.skill`](./ai-readiness-check.skill)
2. Open Claude → Settings → Capabilities → Install skill
3. Start a new conversation and say: **"AI readiness check"**

---

## What comes next

If you want to go deeper, run the [BoS OS Bootstrap](https://github.com/BoSMark/BoS_OS_Start) skill. It builds a picture of your company from public information — so the BoS OS can tell you specifically where AI will help your business, not just suggest what's generally possible.

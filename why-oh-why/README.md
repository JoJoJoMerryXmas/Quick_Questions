# Why Oh Why

**The question:** What are you actually trying to achieve — underneath what you just asked for?

This is a 5–10 minute conversation that takes whatever you bring — a request, a problem, a feature idea, a hire, a decision — and asks "why" repeatedly until it surfaces what you're actually trying to achieve. Most of the time, the thing you asked for first is a proxy for something further up the chain.

You get a WhyOhWhy Brief at the end: what you said you wanted, the ladder of "whys" that got asked, what you actually learned, and the gap between the two.

---

## Install

1. Download [`why-oh-why.skill`](./why-oh-why.skill)
2. Open Claude → Settings → Capabilities → Install skill
3. Start a new conversation and say: **"why oh why"**

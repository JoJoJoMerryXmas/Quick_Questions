# Founder Replaceability Check

**The question:** Could your business run without you — and if not, what would it take?

This is a 5–10 minute conversation that surfaces where you're the single point of failure, names the pattern, and tells you what to do about it.

Most founders know they're the bottleneck. Few have named exactly where, or decided what to do about it. You get a Replaceability Brief at the end: your dependency list, your pattern, and a clear starting point for fixing it.

---

## Install

1. Download [`founder-replaceability-check.skill`](./founder-replaceability-check.skill)
2. Open Claude → Settings → Capabilities → Install skill
3. Start a new conversation and say: **"founder replaceability"**

---

## The eight patterns

Every founder who can't step back fits one of these:

- **The Firefighter** — your team handles tasks; you handle everything that matters
- **The Invisible Glue** — nothing officially requires you, but everything informally does
- **The Strategic Bottleneck** — operations run fine; the real calls still all come through you
- **The Rainmaker** — your team delivers; only you can sell
- **The Brand** — customers are buying you, not the business
- **The Fixer** — the business looks independent until something breaks
- **The Connector** — the external relationships that matter all belong to you personally
- **The Product Brain** — the team builds; only you know what to build next, and why

The skill will name which one fits — and what to do about it.
